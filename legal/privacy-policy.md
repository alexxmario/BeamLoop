# BeamLoop Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 20 July 2026_

BeamLoop ("BeamLoop", "we", "us") lets you publish one piece of content to
several social platforms at once. This policy explains what we collect, why,
and who we share it with. We've tried to keep it plain.

## Who we are

BeamLoop is operated by **[LEGAL ENTITY / YOUR NAME]**, contactable at
**[SUPPORT EMAIL]**. If you have any question about your data, email us there.

## What we collect

- **Account details.** Your email address and a password. Passwords are never
  stored in plain text — they are salted and hashed (scrypt) before storage.
- **Connected social accounts.** When you link a platform (TikTok, Instagram,
  YouTube, Facebook, X, Threads, Discord, or Telegram), we store the access
  tokens or credentials needed to post on your behalf. For Discord and Telegram
  this is a webhook URL or bot token that you provide.
- **Content you publish.** The videos, photos, captions, titles, and delivery
  times you choose. Scheduled media is retained until delivery; it is then
  stored only transiently for retries and deleted after the retry window.
- **Operational logs.** Standard server logs (timestamps, request paths, error
  details) used to run and secure the service.

We do **not** collect location, contacts, advertising identifiers, or analytics
beyond what is described above.

## How we use it

- To authenticate you and keep your session active.
- To publish the content you ask us to publish, to the platforms you choose.
- To let you retry a post that partially failed.
- To secure the service (e.g. rate-limiting abusive traffic) and diagnose
  errors.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

## Who we share it with

- **Post for Me** (our publishing provider) receives your content, connection
  tokens, and an internal account identifier so it can deliver posts to each
  platform. See Post for Me's own privacy terms at
  <https://www.postforme.dev>.
- **The social platforms you connect.** When you publish, your content goes to
  the platforms you selected, subject to their own terms and privacy policies.
- **Hosting and infrastructure providers** that run our servers.

We disclose data to no one else except where required by law.

## Data retention

- Account data is kept until you delete your account.
- Scheduled media is kept until delivery, then for up to seven days to support
  retries before it is deleted.
- Server logs are retained for a limited period for security and debugging.

## Your choices

- **Disconnect a platform** at any time from within the app; we remove the
  stored credentials for it.
- **Delete your account** in the Connections tab. We will delete your
  account details and connection credentials. Content already published to a
  third-party platform is governed by that platform and must be removed there.

## Children

BeamLoop is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your
country), and we do not knowingly collect their data.
